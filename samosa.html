Samosa Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3-4 medium potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 1 cup green peas
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 2 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon ginger, grated
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon coriander seeds, crushed
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the all-purpose flour with a pinch of salt and enough water to make a stiff dough. Cover and let it rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter. Add chopped onions, green chilies, and ginger. Saut√© until onions turn translucent.
  3. Add crushed coriander seeds, turmeric powder, garam masala, and salt. Mix well.
  4. Add mashed potatoes and green peas. Mix until well combined. Cook for 3-4 minutes. Remove from heat and let it cool.
  5. Divide the dough into equal-sized balls. Roll out each ball into a small circle.
  6. Cut each circle in half. Take one half and fold it into a cone shape. Seal the edges using water.
  7. Fill the cone with the potato mixture. Seal the top of the cone by pressing the edges together.
  8. Repeat the process for the remaining dough and filling.
  9. Heat oil in a deep frying pan. Fry the samosas until golden brown and crispy.
  10. Remove from oil and drain on paper towels. Serve hot with chutney or sauce of your choice.
